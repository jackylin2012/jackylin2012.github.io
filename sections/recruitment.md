### PhD Information

I am looking for PhD students in the area of Financial Text Analysis and LLM.

Here are the general requirements:
1. Strong academic background.
2. Highly motivated and is able to work independently.
3. Good command of English (spoken and academic writing).
4. Good programing skills (e.g., Python) and experienced with statistical software (e.g., Stata).

### Scholarship

Qualified students will be recommended for the [UM PhD Scholarship](https://grs.um.edu.mo/index.php/prospective-students/phd-funding/um-phd-scholarship/).
PI funding is also available.

Please send me your resume and other materials if you are interested.
Please understand that only shortlisted candidates will be contacted for an interview.
